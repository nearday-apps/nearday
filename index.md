# Privacy Policy — NearDay

*Last updated: June 2026*

## Overview

NearDay ("we", "the app") is built to respect your privacy. This policy explains what data is collected and how it is used.

---

## Data We Collect

### Data stored only on your device

All countdown data you create — titles, dates, images, checklists, and notification preferences — is stored **exclusively on your device** using Android's local Room database and DataStore. We do not transmit this data to any server we operate.

### Device gallery access (optional)

If you choose to select an image from your device gallery, the app reads that image file locally. It is not uploaded anywhere.

### Notifications

If you enable event reminders, the app schedules local `AlarmManager` alarms on your device. No notification data leaves your device.

---

## Data We Do NOT Collect

- We do not collect your name, email address, or account information.
- We do not use analytics SDKs (no Firebase, no Crashlytics, no Amplitude).
- We do not serve ads.
- We do not make any network requests other than those initiated by Google Play for billing.
- We do not sell or share any data with third parties.

---

## Google Play Billing

If you purchase the Pro upgrade, the transaction is processed entirely by Google Play. We receive only a purchase token to verify your purchase status. We do not see or store your payment information.

---

## Permissions Explained

| Permission | Why it's needed |
|---|---|
| `POST_NOTIFICATIONS` | Required on Android 13+ to show event reminder notifications |
| `READ_MEDIA_IMAGES` / `READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE` | To let you pick a photo from your gallery as a countdown background |
| `USE_EXACT_ALARM` | To fire countdown notifications at the exact scheduled time |
| `RECEIVE_BOOT_COMPLETED` | To re-schedule alarms after the device restarts |
| `VIBRATE` | To vibrate when a countdown expires |

---

## Children's Privacy

NearDay is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.
